About Me

Hi, I'm Hunter Heffernan, I am from Roseville, Minnesota, and this Spring I will have finished my undergraduate at the University of St. Thomas with a B.S. in Computer Science and minors in Art History and Data Science.

After graduation, I plan to pursue a career in software development, specializing in full-stack development.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending time outside. Over my time spent at University, I have gotten into hiking, camping, and fishing. This summer I got to experience all three when I went on a camping trip staying at Lake Itasca State Park as well as Voyagers National Park near Kabetogama. If I'm not outside or coding, odds are I'm spending time with my family and my two cats.
